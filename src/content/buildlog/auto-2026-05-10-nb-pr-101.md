The validator in #96 only allowed paths under `\$HOME`. That blocks Luna's natural flow: when she downloads a Drive file via `download_file_content`, Python's `tempfile.NamedTemporaryFile` lands it at `/var/folders/...` on macOS (resolves to `/private/var/folders/...`) — outside `\$HOME`, so the attachment never went…
