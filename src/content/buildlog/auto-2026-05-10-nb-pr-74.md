Closes a manual step that was slowing tonight's Luna recruitment. When the recruiter's `create_agent` action carries a `client_id` field, `agent_builder.py` now also appends the entry to `scripts/discord_bot/agents.json` so the daemon picks up the new bot on next reload — no hand-edit required.
